Evalution and Administration

Recent News

Evaluation and Administration handles the legally mandated evaluation and assessment of student achievement and leads program and staff evaluation efforts. The Evaluation and Administration department is currently dealing with a wave of scandals related to recent investigative reporting from the local paper - Hopetopian Times - that revealed a subset of teachers may have manipulated student tests to improve assessment scores. Internal investigations have also recently revealed that a large charter school operator has been misleading auditors on the expenditures of funds with the president of the operator taking an outsized share of public payments as salary and utilizing under-qualified teachers. The mayor is taking increasing notice of the series of scandals and expects remedies. 9/12
